## Getting Started

### Starting Your Adventure

1. **Name your trainer**
2. **Choose your starter**:
   - **Flameo** (Fire) - High Speed, Strong Attack
   - **Aquabit** (Water) - Balanced stats, Good Defense
   - **Leaflet** (Grass) - Balanced all-around

3. **First Steps**:
   - You start at the Healing House (H)
   - Walk through grass (") to encounter wild creatures
   - Catch creatures to build your party
   - Level up by battling

## Map & Locations

### Map Legend
- `@` - Your position
- `#` - Walls (impassable)
- `~` - Water (impassable)
- `.` - Path (safe, no encounters)
- `"` - Grass (30% encounter rate)
- `H` - Healing House (restore HP and PP)
- `B` - Boss Area (MEGA DRAGON)

### Encounter Zones

**North Grass** (Top area, Levels 3-7):
- Flameo, Aquabit, Leaflet, Sparky

**Mid Grass** (Middle area, Levels 5-10):
- Flameo, Aquabit, Leaflet, Sparky, Rockhead

**South Grass** (Bottom area, Levels 8-12):
- Rockhead, Windpuff, Toxifrog, Sparky

## Advanced Strategy

### Team Building
1. **Balanced Team**: Mix of types for coverage
2. **Type Synergy**: Cover each other's weaknesses
3. **Level Distribution**: Keep all creatures at similar levels

### Battle Tips
1. **Switch creatures** when at a type disadvantage
2. **Save high PP moves** for tough battles
3. **Use type advantage** for 2x damage
4. **Critical hits** can turn the tide of battle

### Boss Preparation
- **Recommended Level**: 30-35
- **Recommended Party**: 4-6 fully evolved creatures
- **Stock Up**: Visit Healing House before the fight
- **Type Coverage**: Bring Water/Rock types (Fire is weak to Water)

### Grinding Spots
- **Early**: North Grass (quick encounters)
- **Mid**: South Grass (higher level creatures)
- **Pre-Boss**: Grind to Lv.30+ in South Grass

### Efficient Leveling
1. Battle every wild encounter
2. Focus on catching new creatures early
3. Use type advantages for faster battles
4. Return to Healing House frequently
